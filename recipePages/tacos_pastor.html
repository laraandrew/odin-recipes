Tacos Pastor Recipe

This is a famous mexican dish that often can be found being cut off of a "trompo"

Ingredients:

  • Tortillas (maize o harina)
  • Pork
  • Lime
  • Cilantro
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Pineapple
  • Guajillo Chilies

Steps:

  1. Marinate the thinly sliced pork in a blend of guajillo chilies, onion, garlic, vinegar, cumin, oregano, cloves, salt, and pepper for at least 2 hours, or overnight.
  2. Cook the marinated pork on a vertical rotisserie, spit, or skewers until it's cooked through and slightly crispy.
  3. Slice the cooked pork thinly.
  4. Assemble the tacos by serving the pork on warm corn tortillas.
  5. Top each taco with diced pineapple, chopped onions, and cilantro.
  6. Squeeze fresh lime juice over the tacos for added flavor.
