Tacos Pastor Recipe

This is a famous mexican dish that often can be found being cut off of a "trompo"

Ingredients:

  • Tortillas (maize o arina)
  • Pork
  • Sauce

Steps:

  1. Cook meat and sauce
  2. Warm up tortillas on the stove
  3. Place meat and condiments on tortilla served with lime
