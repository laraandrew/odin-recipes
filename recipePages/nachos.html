Nacho Recipe

This is a typical mexican dish that happens to be my favorite of all time

Ingredients:

  • Nacho chips
  • Cheese of choice
  • Jalapenos
  • Chicken/beef
  • Tomatoes
  • Salsa
  • Beans
  • Sour cream

Steps:

  1. Cook preferred meat - my favorite is to airfry chicken
  2. Melt cheese in microwave or boil - boil tin preferred
  3. Place chips on metal baking tray and add: cooked beans, cooked tomatoes, fresh Jalapenos
  4. Place cooked meat and cheese on chips and place into oven at 400 degrees faherenheit for 5 min
  5. Take tray out of the oven and add sour cream and salsa to taste
  6. Serve and enjoy!
