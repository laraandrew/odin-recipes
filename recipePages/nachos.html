Nacho Recipe

This is a typical mexican dish that happens to be my favorite of all time

Ingredients:

  • Nacho chips
  • cheese of choice
  • jalapenos
  • chicken/beef

Steps:

  1. Cook preferred meat
  2. Melt cheese in microwave or boil
  3. Place cooked meat and cheese on chips and place into oven at 400 for 5 min
