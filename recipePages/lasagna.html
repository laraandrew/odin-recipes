Lasagna Recipe

This is a typical italian dish that mexicans love to make

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Mozzerella
  • Red Sauce
  • Ground Beef
  • Olive Oil (or preference)

Steps:

  1. Set out all the ingredients at room temperature
  2. Boil lasagna noodles until al dente. Drain and rinse.
  3. Brown ground beef with onion and garlic.
  4. Add tomatoes, tomato paste, sugar, and spices. Simmer.
  5. Mix ricotta, mozzarella, Parmesan, parsley, and egg.
  6. Layer noodles, meat sauce, cheese mixture.
  7. Repeat layers, finish with noodles, sauce, and cheese.
  8. Bake at 375°F (190°C) until golden. Let it rest before serving.
